Learning Goals:

  • Understand Diel Vertical Migration (DVM), the largest daily animal migration on Earth.
  • Discover how deep-sea animals avoid predators by day and feed by night.

  • Act as the Circadian Rhythm! Use the DAY / NIGHT switch to keep your swarm's Hunger and Safety meters balanced. Survive 3 cycles to win!

The "False Bottom"

During WWII, Navy sonar operators noticed the ocean floor seemed to rise toward the surface at night. They soon realized it wasn't the seafloor at all—it was billions of migrating animals bouncing sound waves! This moving layer is known as the Deep Scattering Layer (DSL).
